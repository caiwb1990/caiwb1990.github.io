lua数组

数组定义 

students = {"aa", "bb", "cc"}

数组的3种遍历方式： 

for i,v in ipairs(students) do  
  print(i ..":" ..v )  
end  

for k,v in pairs(students) do  
  print(k ..":" ..v )  
end  

for i = 1, #students do  
  print(i.. ":".. students[i])  
end
